Phil McAleer appointed SSI Fellow

2020-01-10

Dr Phil McAleer was named as one of the 2020 Software Sustainability Institute Fellows. He was one of 18 fellows chosen from diverse fields, such as Medicine and dentistry; Biological sciences; Physical sciences; Mathematical sciences; Engineering and technology; Computer science; Communications; Histoy; Philosophy; and Veterinary science.

The Fellowship Programme run by the Software Sustainability Institute provides funding for individuals who want to improve how research software is done in their domains and/or area of work. Being a Fellow also helps develop skills and knowledge in their area of software sustainability, empowers them to speak about software sustainability issues and network with like-minded individuals from a wide variety of research areas.
